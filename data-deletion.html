Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: September 6, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your data from TERMPROVIT S.R.L.'s systems, please follow these steps:

1. Automatic Data Deletion

When you request account deletion, we automatically:

  • Stop processing your personal data
  • Begin the process of deleting stored data within 30 days
  • Remove your account information from our systems

2. Manual Data Deletion Request

To request immediate data deletion, please contact us with the following information:

Email Request to: termprovit@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - TERMPROVIT S.R.L.

Include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

3. What Data We Delete

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your contact information
  • Order history and transaction data
  • Communication history
  • Any stored preferences or settings
  • Account and authentication data

4. Timeline

We will process your deletion request within:

  • Immediate: Access tokens and active connections
  • 7 days: Personal data and content
  • 30 days: Backup and archived data

5. Confirmation

Once your data has been deleted, we will send you a confirmation email to verify the deletion is complete.

6. Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain data only when required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as:

  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Anonymized usage statistics (with all personal identifiers removed)
